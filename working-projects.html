Research Analytics Dashboard: Citation Impact Tracking

Research Objective: Develop a comprehensive analytics platform for tracking research impact metrics and citation dynamics across academic outputs.

Methodological Approach: Automated data collection from academic databases, real-time citation tracking, and impact factor analysis using bibliometric indicators.

Current Status: Operational dashboard tracking 15+ research outputs with automated GitHub Actions for metric updates and citation momentum analysis.

Visual Saliency Analysis: Thumbnail Optimization Framework

Research Objective: Investigate the relationship between visual saliency patterns and click-through rates in digital marketing contexts.

Methodological Approach: Computer vision algorithms for saliency detection, mixed-effects modeling for click prediction, and A/B testing validation framework.

Current Status: Manuscript under review at Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition conference, with preliminary results showing 23% improvement in CTR prediction accuracy.

Computer Vision Saliency Detection Mixed-Effects Models

Contextual Bandit Optimization: Dynamic Budget Allocation

Research Objective: Develop an adaptive optimization framework for real-time marketing budget allocation across multiple channels.

Methodological Approach: Contextual bandit algorithms with Thompson sampling, multi-armed bandit optimization, and regret minimization techniques.

Current Status: Algorithm validation completed with synthetic data, preparing for field deployment with demand-side platform integration.

Contextual Bandits Thompson Sampling Regret Minimization

Influencer Marketing Mix Modeling: Cross-Platform Attribution

Research Objective: Quantify the incremental impact of influencer marketing across multiple social media platforms using hierarchical Bayesian modeling.

Methodological Approach: Hierarchical Bayesian models with platform-specific priors, cross-platform attribution modeling, and posterior predictive checks.

Current Status: Data preprocessing completed, hierarchical priors specified, model fitting scheduled for Q1 2025 with cross-validation framework.

Hierarchical Bayes Cross-Platform Analysis Attribution Modeling
Simulation Validation

Dynamic Pricing Optimization: Q-Learning Framework

Research Objective: Develop a reinforcement learning framework for dynamic pricing optimization in quick-commerce environments.

Methodological Approach: Q-learning algorithms with epsilon-greedy exploration, demand forecasting integration, and synthetic demand shock validation.

Current Status: Algorithm validated against synthetic demand scenarios, field deployment planning with grocery retail partner in progress.

Q-Learning Reinforcement Learning Demand Forecasting

News Sentiment Dynamics: System Dynamics Modeling

Research Objective: Model the temporal dynamics of news sentiment and its impact on brand search behavior using system dynamics approaches.

Methodological Approach: Monte Carlo simulation engine, sentiment trajectory analysis, and policy implications modeling for crisis response.

Current Status: Simulation engine operational, manuscript drafting focused on theoretical contributions and managerial implications.
