Vision & Values

My research philosophy centers on bridging theoretical rigor with practical impact, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, and advancing responsible AI applications in marketing and social systems.

Evidence-Based Managerial Science

I aim to fuse behavioural theory with computational rigour so that every model translates into actionable uplift or risk diagnostics for practitioners. Future work will formalise this bridge through joint labâ€“industry consortia.

Interdisciplinary Fusion

I routinely combine marketing, information-systems, and computer-science lenses, believing that multi-perspective triangulation yields more robust insights. Planned collaborations extend this synthesis to public-policy and ethics scholars.

Data-Spectrum Triangulation

I integrate experiments, digital traces, and qualitative diagnostics to balance internal and ecological validity. My next goal is to embed adaptive sampling so data collection co-evolves with emerging evidence gaps.

Theory-Driven Engineering

I open-source reproducible code artefacts that operationalise abstract constructs, enabling cumulative science and classroom adoption. Forthcoming releases will package these tools as modular pip libraries.

Executive Education & Advisory Impact

I deliver AI bootcamps and Bayesian decision-labs that equip senior managers to act on analytic insight. Consulting engagements focus on diagnosing marketing-mix inefficiencies and guiding data-driven culture shifts.

Societal Stewardship

I embed fairness constraints and bias audits into recommender and moderation algorithms, mitigating polarisation risks identified in prior research. Future work targets scalable ethics-by-design toolkits for organisations of all sizes.
