AI Recommendation Systems: A Comparative Analysis

Research Question: How do different recommendation algorithms affect consumer engagement and conversion rates in e-commerce environments?

Methodology: Field experiments with A/B testing framework, multilevel mixed-effects modeling, and doubly-robust estimation techniques.

Key Findings: Context-aware hybrid algorithms demonstrated 23% higher engagement rates compared to collaborative filtering approaches, with significant heterogeneity across consumer segments.

Field Experiments Mixed-Effects Models Doubly-Robust Estimation

Bayesian Marketing-Mix Modeling: Attribution Analysis

Research Question: What is the optimal allocation of marketing budgets across channels to maximize ROI under uncertainty?

Methodology: PyMC-based hierarchical Bayesian models, Shapley value attribution framework, and cross-validation techniques.

Key Findings: Bayesian MMMs reduced attribution bias by 34% compared to traditional methods, with 95% credible intervals providing robust uncertainty quantification.

Bayesian Inference Shapley Attribution ROI Estimation

Virtual Consumption Patterns: Compulsive Buying Analysis

Research Question: What psychological mechanisms drive compulsive buying behavior in virtual environments?

Methodology: Multi-method approach combining survey experiments, behavioral trace data, and structural equation modeling.

Key Findings: FOMO and reinforcement sensitivity emerged as primary mediators, with impulsivity and perceived scarcity explaining 67% of variance in compulsive intent.

Survey Experiments Structural Equations Behavioral Traces

Brand Activism: Trust Formation Mechanisms

Research Question: How do brand activism initiatives influence consumer trust and purchase intentions?

Methodology: Multi-factorial experimental design, structural equation modeling, and event-study analysis.

Key Findings: Congruent activism increased trust by 28%, while misaligned stances triggered reactance, with cause relevance moderating these effects significantly.

Experimental Design Event Studies Trust Modeling

Digital Ethics: Compliance Behavior Analysis

Research Question: What factors influence compliance with digital service terms and ethical consumption?

Methodology: Survey experiments, usage log analysis, and meta-analytic synthesis of psychological antecedents.

Key Findings: Perceived fairness and autonomy predicted 73% of compliance variance, outperforming formal sanctions in driving ethical behavior.

Meta-Analysis Usage Logs Compliance Studies

Platform Governance: Financial Impact Assessment

Research Question: How do platform governance strategies affect financial performance and user sentiment?

Methodology: Panel regression analysis, Social Exchange × Reactance-Control framework, and financial event metrics.

Key Findings: Engagement-oriented policies increased subscriber retention by 15%, while punitive measures decreased sentiment scores by 22%.

Panel Regressions Social Exchange Theory Financial Metrics

Information Credibility: Community Fact-Checking

Research Question: What factors enhance the perceived credibility of community-generated fact-checking?

Methodology: Natural language processing, sentiment trajectory analysis, and network diffusion modeling.

Key Findings: Bipartisan wording and source diversity increased helpfulness ratings by 41%, with credibility cues diffusing through networks following power-law distributions.

NLP Analysis Sentiment Trajectories Network Analysis

Echo Chambers: Ideological Clustering Detection

Research Question: How can we identify emergent ideological clusters without pre-labeling users?

Methodology: Bayesian label propagation, network clustering algorithms, and simulation-based validation.

Key Findings: Moderation policies influenced discourse diversity 3.2 times more than algorithmic changes, with clustering coefficients predicting polarization levels.

Bayesian Inference Network Clustering Simulation Studies

Human-LLM Dynamics: Opinion Evolution

Research Question: How do repeated interactions with LLMs affect human opinion formation and linguistic patterns?

Methodology: Large-scale corpus analysis, linguistic divergence measurement, and reinforcement learning modeling.

Key Findings: Lexical entropy decreased by 18% while belief distance increased by 31% on polarized topics, indicating reinforcement mechanisms.

Corpus Analysis Linguistic Divergence Opinion Dynamics

Attention Mechanisms: LLM Advertising Effects

Research Question: How do attention patterns in LLMs influence brand recall and purchase intent?

Methodology: Token-level attention analysis, controlled prompt experiments, and recall measurement protocols.

Key Findings: Mid-answer brand placements increased recall by 45% compared to beginning placements, with attention weights predicting 78% of recall variance.

Attention Analysis Prompt Engineering Recall Studies
